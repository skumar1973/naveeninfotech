    The Sarayu Ayodhya

    Sarayu River, NH 27, Ayodhya

    Description

    The Sarayu Ayodhya, by the esteemed Abhinandan Lodha Group, in partnership with Hoabl Land Development, presents a distinctive layout and master plan that embodies luxury and serenity.

    THE SARAYU AYODHYA is Ayodhya’s First 7-star mixed-use development near the holy Sarayu River, featuring an integrated 5-star Hotel. It is conveniently located just 15 minutes from the Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Temple and 20-30 minutes from Maharishi Valmiki International Airport.

    In addition to residential plots, the development boasts a 5-star Palace by The Leela Palaces Hotels & Resorts, in partnership with Hoabl. The Leela Palaces Hotels & Resorts will serve the development's clubhouse.

    Ayodhya is on its way to becoming the Global Spiritual Capital with planned transformation to cater to the tremendous growth in its future. This futuristic masterpiece is being designed to consider the spiritual and tourist needs of the residents sustainably. 10 crore annual tourists are expected to arrive at Ayodhya by 2050.

     

    Know about the Developer of The Saarayu Ayodhya

    The House of Abhinandan Lodha is India’s largest branded consumer-tech land developer in INDIA, making purchasing land easy, safe, and transparent. They have over 5000+ happy customers worldwide, who have invested in land at locations like Dapoli & Anjarle on the Konkan coast, Goa, Alibaug, and Foothills of Matheran. They have over 1 cr. sq. ft. under development and have sold 50 Lakh + sq. ft.
