    Description

    Welcome to M3M Altitude, Sector 65, Gurgaon – A New Benchmark in Luxury Living

    Discover the epitome of ultra-luxury living at M3M Altitude at Golf Estate, a newly launched residential masterpiece located on the prestigious Golf Course Road Extension in Sector 65, Gurugram. This high-rise development redefines sophistication and modernity, offering unmatched specifications and world-class amenities. Experience a lifestyle that rises above the ordinary, where every detail is crafted for excellence.

    Project Overview

    Spread across approximately 5 acres of verdant landscapes, M3M Altitude comprises three high-rise towers designed to exude grandeur. This RERA-approved project (RERA number 48 of 2024) features 4BHK ultra-luxury apartments and penthouses, offering only four units per core to ensure unparalleled privacy and exclusivity.

    The apartments, ranging from 3755 to 4270 sq. ft., are thoughtfully designed for maximum space efficiency and adorned with premium interiors that elevate their aesthetic appeal. Whether for living or investment, this project promises to fulfill your aspirations for a luxurious lifestyle.

    World-Class Amenities and Features

    M3M Altitude offers a host of premium amenities to complement its residents' modern and luxurious lifestyles. Designed by UHA London, one of the leading architectural firms globally, the project combines innovative design with functionality.

    Residents can enjoy an array of conveniences right at their doorstep, including recreational facilities, lush green landscapes, and meticulously crafted interiors that reflect sophistication and comfort.

    Prime Location

    Strategically situated at the heart of Gurugram on Golf Course Road Extension, M3M Altitude provides seamless connectivity to major hubs in the city and beyond. Key highlights of its location include:

    • Proximity to Major Landmarks: Close to top corporate offices, educational institutions, shopping destinations, hospitals, and commercial centers.
    • Excellent Connectivity: Easy access to Golf Course Road, Sohna Road, SPR Road, Cyber Hub, and Dwarka Expressway.
    • Convenient Commutes: Just 30 minutes’ drive to IGI Airport, 12 minutes to NH-48, and 8 minutes to the Rapid Metro.

    A Serene Retreat Amidst Urban Vibrance

    For those seeking tranquility without compromising on connectivity, M3M Altitude offers a perfect blend of lush greenery and urban sophistication. Designed as golf-themed residences, the project provides an escape from the city's hustle and bustle while ensuring you remain close to everything you need.

     

    Why Choose M3M Altitude?

    Whether you're searching for your dream home or a high-potential investment, M3M Altitude is a stellar choice. Its prime location, impeccable design, and luxurious amenities make it a standout project in Gurugram’s real estate market.

     

    About M3M Group

    M3M India has earned a reputation as one of the most trusted and innovative real estate developers in India. With a focus on creating projects that embody luxury, innovation, and sustainability, M3M delivers exceptional living and commercial spaces that redefine urban living.
