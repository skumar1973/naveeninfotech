    Privacy Policy

    We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy will explain how we collect, use, and handle your information when you access or interact with our website.

    Information We Collect

    Personal Information:

    Usage Data:

    We may collect non-personally identifiable information about how you interact with our website. This includes your IP address, browser type, the pages you visit, the time and date of your visits, and other similar information.

    How We Use Your Information

    Fulfilling Services:

    We use your personal information to provide you with the services and information you request, such as property listings, newsletters, or updates.
